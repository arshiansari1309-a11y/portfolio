Project

Everglow Jewels – Case Study

Project Title: Scaling Everglow Jewels with Social Media & Meta Ads

Brand Overview

Everglow Jewels is a modern jewellery brand focused on anti-tarnish, stylish, and affordable jewellery for daily wear.

Target Audience

  • Women (18–35 years)
  • Fashion & accessories lovers
  • Online shoppers
  • College students & working women

Problem

  • Low engagement on posts
  • No structured ad campaigns
  • Lack of consistent branding

Strategy Implemented

Content Strategy:

  • Minimal & classy creatives
  • Focus on anti-tarnish benefit
  • POV & problem-solution reels

Ad Strategy:

  • Awareness → Engagement → Conversion funnel
  • Ad creatives & captions
  • Audience targeting

Branding:

  • Clean & premium look
  • Focus on trust & durability

Ad Copy

Primary Text: Tired of jewellery that fades in weeks?

Switch to anti-tarnish pieces that stay shiny for months ✨

Headline: Jewellery That Lasts

Results

  • Improved engagement
  • Better audience understanding
  • Strong base for scaling ads

Key Learnings

  • Creative quality matters
  • Right targeting is crucial
  • Consistency builds trust
