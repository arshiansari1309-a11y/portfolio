# EVERGLOW JEWELS  
## Brand Theory & Identity Guidelines

---

# 1. Brand Foundation

## Brand Name
**EverGlow Jewels**

### Meaning Behind the Name
The name “EverGlow” symbolizes:
- Timeless beauty
- Lasting elegance
- Inner radiance
- Emotional memories attached to jewellery

The word “Glow” reflects confidence, femininity, and luxury, while “Ever” communicates durability and timelessness.

The brand represents jewellery that is not only worn but emotionally connected to personal stories and milestones.

---

# 2. Brand Vision

> To create elegant, timeless jewellery that allows every woman to express her individuality with confidence and grace.

---

# 3. Brand Mission

EverGlow Jewels aims to bridge the gap between:
- Luxury aesthetics
- Affordability
- Handcrafted elegance
- Emotional storytelling

The brand focuses on creating anti-tarnish jewellery pieces that feel premium, modern, and deeply personal.

---

# 4. Brand Philosophy

## “Jewellery Should Tell a Story”

EverGlow Jewels believes jewellery is:
- More than an accessory
- A reflection of emotion
- A symbol of memories
- A form of self-expression

Every piece should make the customer feel:
- Elegant
- Confident
- Feminine
- Valuable

# 5. Target Audience

### Lifestyle Traits

# 11. Typography Theory

Typography defines luxury perception.

EverGlow requires:
- High-fashion serif headings
- Clean modern sans-serif body text

---

## Heading Font
# Cormorant Garamond

### Why?
- Elegant curves
- Editorial luxury appearance
- Fashion-magazine aesthetics
- Feminine sophistication

### Usage
- Hero headings
- Section titles
- Luxury statements

---

## Body Font
# Inter

### Why?
- Clean readability
- Modern interface balance
- Minimal and professional

### Usage
- Paragraphs
- Buttons
- Navigation
- Product descriptions

---

# 12. Typography Hierarchy

## Hero Heading

```css
font-size: 72px;
font-weight: 600;
line-height: 1.1;
letter-spacing: -1px;
```

---

## Section Headings

```css
font-size: 48px;
font-weight: 600;
```

---

## Body Text

```css
font-size: 16px;
line-height: 1.8;
```

# 14. UI/UX Brand Theory

Luxury UI is based on:
- Space
- Simplicity
- Emotion
- Focus

---

## Core Principles

### 1. Less Clutter
Luxury interfaces never feel crowded.

---

### 2. Large White Space
Spacing creates premium perception.

Recommended:

```css
padding-top: 120px;
padding-bottom: 120px;
```

---

### 3. Soft Interactions
Animations should be:
- Slow
- Smooth
- Elegant

Avoid flashy transitions.

---

### 4. Cinematic Photography
Use:
- Warm lighting
- Silk textures
- Marble backgrounds
- Gold reflections
- Natural skin tones

---

# 15. Photography Direction

Photography should communicate:
- Warmth
- Luxury
- Femininity
- Editorial fashion aesthetics

---

## Ideal Image Elements

- Velvet textures
- Gold reflections
- Soft shadows
- Neutral backgrounds
- Close-up craftsmanship shots

---

## Avoid

- Overexposed white images
- Harsh lighting
- Oversaturated colors
- Cheap stock photos

---

# 16. Product Card Theory

Current product cards feel generic ecommerce.

Luxury product cards should:
- Use larger images
- Reduce border heaviness
- Add subtle shadows
- Maintain clean typography
- Include hover zoom effects

---

## Recommended Style

```css
border-radius: 20px;
overflow: hidden;
transition: 0.4s ease;
```

---

# 17. Button Design Theory

Luxury buttons should feel:
- Minimal
- Confident
- Premium

---

## Recommended Button Style

```css
background: #C6A14A;
border-radius: 999px;
padding: 14px 32px;
font-weight: 500;
```

---

## Hover Effect

```css
background: #1F1B18;
color: white;
transition: 0.3s ease;
```
